Kerry College

This is a globally recognised accelerated qualification for those who wish to start work as a junior software developer. Successful applicants will complete a 5-day coding challenge and recruitment interview. The course content is extremely practical and is designed to meet current industry needs by developing proficiency in the most sought-after development languages and tools. Learning is achieved through a series of immersive projects supported by lectures, online tutoring, mentoring, webinars and workshops. Learners demonstrate proficiency in Full Stack web development through collaboration with other programmers, through extensive practical work and team exercises and by completing a final portfolio of five project assignments. The course located in Karry College in a peaceful residential area of Tralee town, Co. Kerry, Ireland. Software Development(Full Stack)

It's a key resource within the developer community, known for its large repository of solutions to common (and not-so-common) programming problems.
